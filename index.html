Happiness?

 

where can you find it

 

within

 

really?

 

no, it's Without

It's in God.

Do you Who That Is?

 

the pie in the sky, dream? you, a dreaming soul?

 

not really, not quite. try to look further,

 

You are not God.

 

God is not you.

 

Nor is it me, text, words, letters, punctuation marks.

 

GOD is that which cannot die,

which is the possibility to hope.
